About UFS

Our mission, history, and the team behind the University Forensics Society.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, the University Forensics Society (UFS) began as a small group of passionate students dedicated to the art of debate and public speaking. What started as informal gatherings in campus cafeterias has grown into one of the most respected debate societies in the region.

Over the years, we've hosted numerous inter-university competitions, trained hundreds of students in critical thinking and rhetoric, and represented our university at national and international debate tournaments.

Our alumni network includes lawyers, policymakers, journalists, and business leaders who credit their UFS experience with developing essential communication and analytical skills.

Our Mission

"To foster intellectual growth through structured debate, equipping students with the skills to analyze complex issues, articulate reasoned arguments, and engage in civil discourse."

Public Speaking

Develop confidence and clarity in expressing ideas to diverse audiences.

Critical Analysis

Learn to evaluate arguments, identify logical fallacies, and construct sound reasoning.

Teamwork

Collaborate with peers to research and develop comprehensive arguments.

Global Awareness

Engage with contemporary issues from multiple cultural perspectives.
