Frequently Asked Questions

You can join UFS by filling out our membership application on the Join page. There are no tryouts or prerequisites - we welcome students of all experience levels.

No prior experience is required! We offer workshops and training sessions for beginners, as well as advanced coaching for experienced debaters.

Basic membership is free for all university students. Some special events or tournaments may have participation fees, but we offer need-based waivers.

Most of our events are open to all students, though members get priority registration. Check the Events page for details on each event.
