About UFS

Founded in 2013, the UENR Forensic Society has grown to become the premier debating organization on campus with over 200 active members. Our mission is to raise awareness of social issues through active dialogue.

Key Features:

  • Award-winning coaching staff with national debate experience
  • Training in British Parliamentary debate formats
  • Inclusive community welcoming all skill levels
  • Weekly workshops and practice sessions
